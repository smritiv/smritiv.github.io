Lady Midnight

july 14, 2022

From the iron years then to the new years now,
In a city where all the stories are True:
There thrived a simple choice that you may know
One that most can never undo-
And this choice lived in two bodies
Oh! it must have ailed you too–
For it was a blessing and it was a curse,
In this city where all the stories are True,
But it appeared to folk as neither but a rite
The head and its tail too
Such a rite that the true Angels of heaven
Protected it from wearing through.

So it was for this cause that, from then to now
In this city where all the stories are True,
The people wedged between light and dark:
Reaching and the choice they drew;
Whether to be engulfed in poor but gold-tinted sight
So one may forget about the mildew
Or venture into bright lit beaches with hot
Sand and no lack of rue.

One, for the loss of his love- at the
Betrayal from people he thought he knew
Yes! it was for this cause (as all the folk know,
In this city where all the stories are True)
That he chose the cave of charcoal,
Renouncing his ability to see hue.

Another, out of survival in a city sharp and cunning,
Love for a shadow of the past view,
Need of protection for their light too,
And neither the true Angels of heaven above,
Nor the dishonest warriors of compass new
Could ever stray him away from the life for him
In the dusk where what's said is not True.

Others call them inhuman for they left all in ruin
Oh! how that is askew-
But to the night they are tied by such mortal binds
Oh! how that is askew-
They tell their lies and pull out their knives
For their darlings, their darlings, their lights and their lives,
In the cave in the city True–
In the dusk in the city True.
